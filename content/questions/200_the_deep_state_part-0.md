This opens a new chapter series on MMT & the Deep State.

The notion of the "deep state" is originally leftist critique, not 
right-wing pablum, 
originating form observations of mafia-like state directed 
[activity in Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deep_state_in_Turkey) with covert CIA elements, the _derin devlet_

We will draw upon writing on the fring and outside of academia like,
* [A> Good, “American Exception”]()
* [P. Dale-Scott, “The Deep State”]()
* [“Modern Monetary Theory Without Class Struggle is Just Accounting”](https://realprogressives.substack.com/p/modern-monetary-theory-without-class),

among other references. Citation chasing is not our explicit goal, because we 
are providing an alternative to mainstream narratives.  Maintream opinion 
could easily swap our citations!

The spirit of our inquiry is in aaron Good's ssnese, inhertied from 
C. Wright Mills, whihc is that when so much deepo truith is burided, and 
unwritten, the analysis of power & politics must often use a nagative template 
(look at what was not said or ducmented, or omitted) and must aproach 
polticial analysis fmore _like a crime scene_ than a reliable data gatehring 
technocratic nerd endeavour.
