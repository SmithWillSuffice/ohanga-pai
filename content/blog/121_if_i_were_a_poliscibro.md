but a massive herd of MMT goats proves otherwise, it is possible.

In other words, what is important is not _the_ material conditions, but what 
can be justly accomplished. If we just cannot feed and house everyone then 
we just have to get to work to try feeding and housing everyone. The material 
conditions then fade into background, where they belong, and to the fore is 
justice and fairness, trustworthiness, honesty, compassion and 
kindness --- why?  **_because we can do no better!_**
