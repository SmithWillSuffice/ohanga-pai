## Tinder Fire?

Where is the fire?

One other reason Aaron's work has "excited" me recently, is because 
of the near deafening silence on the recognition of the core problems 
of our governmental systems.

People seem to be blaming the bad apples. But it is a systemic failure, 
requiring radical overhaul, and courageous politicians and public servants 
are needed, and we there to support them fully and I'd say almost 
unconditionally support them.

There is a good to government, what political scientists in Aaron's vein 
refer to as the _Madisonites_. The security state are the _Trumanites_, 
and the deep-state mafiosi (overworld + underworld) are the third part 
of tripartite government.

Only two fo  these are illegitimate aspects of government.

All three exist I believe to some extent in all nations, except maybe 
small island nations.

The one legitimate form of government is what people tend to blame and 
criticize, but this is horrifically misdirected anger. It is blaming the 
vicom almost. Blaming the powerless.

The reason why people forming the nominal democratic state are so 
pathetic (for the most part) is because there is a self-selection 
process in play, the more careerist insipid and feckless "big speakers" 
"little thinkers" are the ones offered up to you to vote for. There is 
also self-sortition once they get into Parliament. The "team players" get 
the juicy portfolios, the more neoliberal you are the closer to the 
Finance Minster.  A totally rotting system, but mainly because of the 
workings of the other two aspects of tripartite government, security 
state and deep sate. So it is wasting your breathe, largely, to point 
fingers at and curse those who happen to rise to the seats in the 
democratic face of government.

If you think, "we have no hope" that is because deep down you know these 
democratic actors are largely just that, mere actors. It is not really 
their fault. They're often just as much victim as you and me. Hey... not 
that I am making excuses for them, that's not what i am doing. I am saying 
activist effort has to get smarter, and divulge the root causes of 
corruption of _our_ government.

We would be half all-right if there only was this one legitimate form 
of government. It is a whole ideology and paradigm that brings the overworld 
and underworld aspects of government into power and all but completely 
dominating the one legitimate form of government, which is the nominal 
democratic state. The nominal democratic states need to be made the real, 
not just the nominal. This can only happen if the tow other blocs of 
government are completely abolished. 

How? Well, that's not for today, but I can tease more writing on this in 
the weeks ahead, which I am sure no one of any significant influence is 
reading, but it's "out there" anyway as evidence that am I still not 
requiring pharmaceutical anti-depressants. But although you may feel of 
no significant influence, you certainly are not when united with other 
workers.

Thus a wieser leftist activism is needed, and towards this end many 
elements of conservative thought are not altogetrh our enemies, provided 
only they are pro-worekr and anti-oligarch. The cultural wars thus have to 
be placed squarely in the background. Why, after all, do women in several 
USA states now not have a legal right of choice? It is not because 
of "conservatism".
